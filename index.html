About Me

Name: Sirun Wu

Born: 2001

I am a creative thinker and hardworking game developer currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Plymouth University. With a strong foundation in software development and a deep passion for storytelling, I strive to build immersive and emotional experiences through games. My academic background has provided me with a solid understanding of systems thinking, project management, and user-focused design—all of which contribute to my development process.

I'm especially drawn to retro, horror, and surreal aesthetics, and take inspiration from games like Bloodborne and The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild. Outside of programming, I spend my time drawing creatures, illustrating characters, and playing bass guitar—creative hobbies that fuel my vision as a developer.
